# EduTrack Teacher Management Portal

## Overview

EduTrack is a Teacher Management Portal designed for small schools, coaching centers, and individual teachers.

The platform replaces manual registers, attendance books, mark sheets, fee records, and paper-based student management with a centralized web application.

---

## Features

### Admin Panel

* Create Teacher Accounts
* Reset Teacher Passwords
* Disable Teacher Accounts
* Delete Teacher Accounts
* View Teacher Statistics

---

### Teacher Panel

Teachers have complete control over their own classroom data.

Features:

* Student Management
* Subject Management
* Attendance Management
* Exam Management
* Marks Entry
* Fee Tracking
* Notes Upload
* Question Paper Upload
* Reports Export
